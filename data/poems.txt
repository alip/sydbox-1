From: woutershep
Date: 2015-04-30
Bug: syd_proc_ppid() breaks for processes with names which have spaces.
Poem:
	-- Pregnant

	drawing curtains
	hiding fetus
	behind venus
	all Night long
	let it flow
	into snow
	crystals in a row
	mothers will bow
	and swallow
	their unborn babies!

		-alip
